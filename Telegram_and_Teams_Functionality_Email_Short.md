## Overview

Two new automated systems are now operational:

1. **Telegram Customer Service Bot** - 24/7 self-service for customers
2. **MS Teams Auto-Reports** - Real-time operational monitoring

---

## 1. TELEGRAM CUSTOMER SERVICE BOT

**Access:** https://t.me/DPDC_customerInfo_bot
**Status:** Live 24/7

### What Customers Can Do

Search `@DPDC_customerInfo_bot` on Telegram, send their Customer/Meter Number, and instantly access:

- **Customer Information** - Name, meter, NOCS, address, balance, last bill date
- **Billing History** - Monthly bills (last month, 6 months, 1 year, or all records)
- **Recharge History** - Payment records with dates and amounts

### Benefits

- 24/7 availability - no login required
- Reduces customer service call volume
- Instant access to account information
- Simple button-based interface

---

## 2. MS TEAMS AUTO-REPORTS

**Status:** Live and sending reports automatically

### Report 1: Batch Monitoring (Every 30 Minutes)

**Shows:**
- Pending IMD records count
- Currently running batches with processing speed (RPS)
- Color-coded performance indicators (Green/Yellow/Red)
- 24-hour statistics

**Alerts:** Immediate notification when batches get stuck or slow down

### Report 2: NOCS Balance Summary (Every 60 Minutes)

**Shows:**
- Total customers across all NOCS areas
- Credit balance (customers with advance payment)
- Due balance (customers with outstanding bills)
- Net balance (overall financial position)

### Benefits

- Proactive issue detection
- Immediate alerts for stuck batches
- No manual monitoring needed
- Historical performance tracking
- Regular financial status updates
