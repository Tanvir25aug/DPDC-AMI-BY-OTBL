## Two New Systems Now Live

### 1. Telegram Customer Service Bot
**Link:** https://t.me/DPDC_customerInfo_bot
**Status:** Live 24/7

**What it does:**
Customers send their Customer/Meter Number and instantly get:
- Customer info (name, meter, NOCS, address, balance)
- Billing history (monthly bills)
- Recharge history (payments)

**Impact:** Reduces call center volume, provides 24/7 self-service

---

### 2. MS Teams Auto-Reports
**Status:** Running automatically

**Two Reports:**

1. **Batch Monitoring** (every 30 min)
   - Shows running batches with speed indicators
   - Alerts if batches get stuck

2. **NOCS Balance Summary** (every 60 min)
   - Total customers and balances across all NOCS
   - Credit/Due amounts

**Impact:** Proactive issue detection, no manual monitoring needed
